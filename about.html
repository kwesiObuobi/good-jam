GoodJam Logo

The logo of GoodJam 2023 was decided by the invited conference speakers for the said conference. After a casual hangout they held in Paris, France, The decided to use the name of the conference as the logo, with multiple colors clipped into the logo text. The idea is to assert that quality music isn't based on a specific race or group of people.
